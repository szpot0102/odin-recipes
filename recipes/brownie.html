Brownie

Brownie is really cheap and easy chocolate cake to make

We need those ingredients:

  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

How to make our brownie

  1. Mix together dry and wet ingredients in two seperate bowls
  2. Mix together dry and wet ingredients together
  3. Pour our cake on the baking pan
  4. Now it's time for baking
